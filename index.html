About Us

Provider of ethical and reliable labour export services.

Vibrant Consultants Immigration Agency is a business entity that specializes in recruiting and supplying workers to employers in foreign countries.We act as intermediaries between job seekers in their home country and employers abroad, facilitating the recruitment process and ensuring compliance with relevant laws and regulations.

We typically provide services such as job placement, visa and work permit assistance, pre-departure training, and logistical support for workers.We play a crucial role in addressing labour market imbalances by matching the supply and demand of workers across different countries and industries.

Our Services

We provides a range of services to both job seekers and employers.

Job Placement

We assist job seekers in finding suitable employment opportunities in foreign countries based on their skills, qualifications, and preferences.

Recruitment and Selection

We actively recruit and screen potential candidates to ensure they meet the requirements of employers. This includes conducting interviews, verifying qualifications, and checking references.

Documentation and Legal Support

We assist both job seekers and employers in navigating the complex paperwork and legal requirements involved in international employment. This may include visa and work permit processing, contract preparation, and compliance with immigration and labour laws.

Re-departure Training

We provide training programs to prepare workers for their overseas employment, which may include language courses, cultural orientation, job-specific skills training, and information on rights and responsibilities abroad.

Travel and Logistics

We handle travel arrangements, including booking flights, arranging accommodation, and organizing transportation to ensure smooth transitions for workers.

Financial Services

We offer services related to remittances, such as facilitating the transfer of wages earned by workers back to their home countries.
